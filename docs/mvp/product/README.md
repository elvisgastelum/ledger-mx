## Product Summary

LedgerMx helps people with irregular income manage finances through:
- Paycheck-based budgeting
- Protected envelopes for earmarked funds
- Debt payoff planning
- Offline transaction capture
- Biweekly income scheduling (every second Friday)
- Three-paycheck month handling (reduced third paycheck)

## Target Users

- Mexican professionals with variable income
- People managing multiple accounts and credit cards
- Users needing offline-first transaction tracking
- Individuals planning debt payoff strategies

## Key Differentiators

1. **Paycheck-centric**: Budget from income occurrences, not monthly averages
2. **Protected envelopes**: Allocate funds for specific purposes
3. **Offline-first**: Capture transactions anywhere, sync later
4. **Clean architecture**: Maintainable, testable, extensible codebase
